ü´Ä What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease refers to various conditions that affect the heart's structure and function. The most common type is coronary artery disease (CAD), which develops when blood vessels supplying the heart become narrowed or blocked.

Common Risk Factors:

  • High Blood Pressure: Forces your heart to work harder
  • High Cholesterol: Can lead to plaque buildup
  • Smoking: Damages blood vessel lining
  • Diabetes: High blood sugar damages vessels
  • Obesity: Increases strain on the heart
  • Physical Inactivity: Weakens cardiovascular system

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning Signs & Symptoms

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in arms, back, neck, or jaw
  • Cold sweats or nausea
  • Irregular heartbeat

Our Mission

We aim to make early heart disease screening accessible to everyone through technology. By combining machine learning with medical knowledge, we provide a preliminary risk assessment that can help individuals take proactive steps toward heart health.
